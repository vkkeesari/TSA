Seasonal Specials

Each season brings fresh and vibrant food into our kitchen. Our amazing chefs always cook up special dishes to highlights the amazing flavors of nature each season has to offer

  • Spring Mango Chaat: A refreshing salad of raw mango, placed among moong beans and pomogranate seeds and drizzled with tangy tamarind dressing
  • Summer Coconut Malai Kofta: A soft kofta made from paneer and summer vegetables, dipped in rich coconut and cashew sauce, drizzled with fresh basil
  • Autumn Pumpkin Sabzi: Tender pumpkin cubes cooked with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dry red chilli, sprinkled with a hint of jaggery and tamarind
  • Winter Kashmiri Rogan Josh(Vegetarian style): A warm, slow cooked curry with lotus stems, paneer, and mashmiri spices, served with a touch of saffron and dried fenugreek leaves
